Quick Turkey Chili

Description

This turkey based chili recipe by the Women's Day Kitchen is a 25 minute recipe and is able to be frozen for up to 3 months! Get ready to make a great tasting meal that you can quickly reheat after a long day or for quick food.

Made with several ingredients, this recipe is a heathier alternative to eating beef-based chili, as it uses lean turkey. Make sure that you have all the following ingredients prepped, nearby, and ready to turn on before you turn on the heat.
